About Us

At Cresenty Studio, we are passionate about game development. We focus on creating games that we think should exist in today's world. By that, we mean games with a deeper meaning and at the same time, very captivating experiences that truly focus on the user. From visuals to soundtracks and the storytelling in those games—breathtaking stuff.

SMALL TEAM

We are a resilient team of two, working tirelessly to make the games you desire come to life. We structure and make decisions together in unison, coming up with spectacular games that leave a lasting impression.
